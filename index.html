DPS Knowledge Park V

About School

Delhi Public School Knowledge Park V is a premier CBSE-affiliated institution dedicated to academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development. The school provides a modern learning environment with smart classrooms, advanced laboratories, a well-stocked library, and excellent sports facilities. It focuses on nurturing students intellectually, physically, and morally, helping them grow into confident and responsible individuals. With experienced faculty and a strong emphasis on values, creativity, and innovation, DPS KP-V prepares students to face future challenges and become successful leaders.
